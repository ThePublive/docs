description: "Overview of Publive's built-in AI capabilities for editors — writing assistance, tag suggestions, and more."
---

Publive includes AI features built directly into the editor to help content teams work faster without interrupting their flow. Each feature operates within a specific field or context, staying out of the way until you need it.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="AI Copilot" icon="sparkles" href="/documentation/concepts-features/ai-copilot">
    Inline writing assistance for Title, Summary, and Meta Description fields — optimise, rewrite, and improve content without leaving the editor.
  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Tag Suggestion" icon="tag" href="/documentation/concepts-features/ai-tag-suggestion">
    Surfaces relevant tags from your publisher's tag library inside the Tags field — click the AI icon to get suggestions based on your post content.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
